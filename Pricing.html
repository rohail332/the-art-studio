FAQ

Common Questions

How do I place an order?

Simply click "Get Started" or visit the Contact page, fill in your project details, and we'll respond within a few hours with next steps.

What file formats will I receive?

Depending on your plan: PNG, JPG for Starter; all formats (SVG, AI, PDF, EPS, PNG, JPG) for Professional and Business plans.

Can I request more revisions?

Yes! Additional revisions beyond your plan limit are available at a small extra fee. Business plan clients get unlimited revisions included.

Is there a money-back guarantee?

We offer unlimited revisions to ensure satisfaction. If we can't deliver what was agreed, we'll provide a full refund.

Do you offer custom quotes?

Absolutely. For large-scale or unique projects, contact us for a custom quote tailored to your specific requirements.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept PayPal, credit/debit cards, bank transfer, and payments via Fiverr and Upwork platforms.
